Questions?

You choose.

When you sign up, you'll get full access to Premium for 7 days. On Day 7, you can subscribe to Plus or Premium to keep all the features, or continue using Elderella for free with our Free plan — no payment required.

Your care information stays safe regardless of which plan you choose, and you'll have access to everything within your new plan's limits.

You pay based on how many elders you're caring for. If you're caring for one parent, you pay for one elder. If you're caring for both parents, you'd pay for two. Each elder needs their own profile — this keeps their medications, appointments, and care details separate and accurate.

Your own profile is always free, and care team members (siblings, aides, etc.) don't pay separately — they're included in your plan.

You can change your plan anytime in the app under Settings > Subscription.

Upgrade: Takes effect immediately. You'll be charged the prorated difference.

Downgrade: Takes effect at your next billing date. You keep your current features until then.

Cancel: You'll move to the Free plan at the end of your billing period. Your data stays safe.

Your care information is not deleted. If you downgrade:

  • Care memory beyond 90 days won't be accessible, but it's not gone. When you ask Ella a question, she'll answer with information from the last 90 days and let you know if there's more relevant care memory available. You can then decide if you want to upgrade to access it.
  • Voice notes longer than 5 minutes are preserved but new ones follow your plan's limit.
  • Extra care team members beyond your plan's limit can still view information but can't be added to new tasks.

You can upgrade again anytime to restore full access.

No. Care team members are included in your plan at no extra cost. When you invite family members, friends, or professional caregivers to your care team, they get access based on the permissions you set — and they don't need their own subscription.

Only the account owner managing the elder's profile pays.

Yes. To transfer billing responsibility:

  1. The current subscriber makes the new person a "Primary Caregiver" on the care team
  2. The current subscriber downgrades to Free
  3. The new Primary Caregiver subscribes to a paid plan

Your care memory and team stay intact throughout the switch.

Your information stays safe. When you cancel, you move to our Free plan — you don't lose access to Elderella.

If you ever want to delete your account entirely, please fill out the form on this page, and we'll permanently remove all your data within 30 days.
