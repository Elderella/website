## How to Use

Copy the prompt below and paste it into Google Gemini (Nano Banana or Nano Banana Pro) to generate an email template design for Elderella.

You can customize the text in the **LAYOUT** section for different emails while keeping the brand identity and design direction consistent.

---

## Prompt

```
Design a single-column email template for Elderella, a caregiving app. This is a welcome
email sent to new users within 1 hour of signing up.

BRAND IDENTITY:
- Brand name: Elderella (with a small purple sparkle/star accent on the "ll")
- Color palette: white (#FFFFFF) background, soft lavender/purple (#EFEEFC), periwinkle
  blue for CTA buttons / links / strength (#5A4FCF), coral for fun (#DC607A), dark
  charcoal (#595959) for body text
- Typography: Merriweather Sans for headlines, Source Sans Pro for body text
- Decorative elements: small purple and/or gold four-pointed sparkle/star accents
  scattered sparingly
- Overall feel: calm, warm, breathing room, like a quiet moment with a cup of tea. NOT
  clinical, NOT corporate. Think cozy blanket, not hospital brochure.

DESIGN DIRECTION:
- The audience is overwhelmed family caregivers. The design should feel like a deep breath.
  Generous whitespace. Soft edges. Rounded corners on all buttons and cards. Nothing
  aggressive or urgent.
- Use warm, muted tones. Avoid harsh contrasts.
- Rounded periwinkle blue CTA buttons with white text, no shadow
- Light lavender sections to break up content blocks
- Small, simple line-art icons next to the two action items (a chat bubble icon for the
  group chat option, a microphone icon for the capture option)
- No hero image -- keep it text-forward and clean, with whitespace as the primary design
  element
- Email width: 600px max, with 40px padding on each side

LAYOUT (top to bottom):

1. HEADER: Elderella logo (left-aligned) small and understated. No navigation links.

2. GREETING BLOCK (white background):
   "Hi [Name]," in serif font, warm charcoal color.

   Body text in sans-serif, regular weight, ~20px, generous line-height (1.5):
   "Ella is ready to start helping. She's good at remembering the things you don't have
   room to hold right now -- the medications, the appointments, the details that slip
   through the cracks when life gets loud."

3. PREMIUM BANNER (soft cream/beige rounded card with no border):
   "You have full Premium access for 7 days. Use Elderella each day this week, and we'll
   double it to 14."
   -- Set in slightly emphasized text (medium weight), centered.

4. SECTION LABEL:
   "Pick whatever feels useful right now:" in Source Sans Pro font, medium size.

5. ACTION CARD 1 (white card with very light lavender left border, rounded corners, subtle
   shadow):
   Icon: small chat bubble line icon in periwinkle
   Title (semi-bold, Source Sans Pro): "Add Ella to your family group chat"
   Body: "If your family already has a text thread about caregiving, add Ella. She'll start
   picking up appointments, medications, and care details from the conversation -- no extra
   work from anyone."

6. ACTION CARD 2 (same card style):
   Icon: microphone icon in purple
   Title (semi-bold, Source Sans Pro): "Capture something before it disappears"
   Body: "Open a chat, type a quick note, record a voice memo, or snap a photo. A
   medication. An appointment. Something the doctor said last week. Ella will have it ready
   when you actually need it."

7. CTA BUTTON (centered):
   Rounded periwinkle with white text: "See what your first minute with Ella looks like"

8. CLOSING (white background):
   "One small action today. That's all it takes." -- in Merriweather Sans, slightly larger,
   centered. This is the emotional anchor of the email.

   Then: "-- The Elderella Team", left-aligned.

9. P.S. LINE:
   "P.S. -- You can also sync your calendar so Ella sees upcoming appointments
   automatically." with "sync your calendar" as a purple underlined text link.

10. FOOTER: Minimal. Muted gray text, small font. Unsubscribe link, company address.
    Elderella logo mark (just the sparkle) centered above footer text.

IMPORTANT STYLE NOTES:
- Everything should feel spacious. Err on the side of too much whitespace.
- Text should feel conversational, not designed. Like a thoughtful note, not a marketing
  blast.
- No stock photography. No gradients. No shadows.
- The two action cards should feel like gentle suggestions, not demands.
- The periwinkle CTA button should be the only bold color pop in the entire email.

Output a complete, polished email template design as a single image at 600px width.
```

## Tips for Adapting This Prompt

- **For a different email**: Replace the text in the LAYOUT section but keep sections 1-10 as the structural template.
- **To add an image**: Replace "No hero image" in Design Direction with a description of the image you want (e.g., "Soft watercolor illustration of hands holding a cup of tea").
- **If text renders poorly**: Regenerate or try Nano Banana Pro for better text fidelity.
