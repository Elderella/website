Manage multiple elders

Keep all your elders in one place, and easily switch between their profiles without losing track of who needs what when.

Know what's next

Your "What's Next" view shows the most pressing tasks and closest events at a glance, so you can stop wondering what you're forgetting.

See upcoming care needs

Sync your calendar and let Elderella line up visits, reminders, and follow-ups so you can see what's coming before it hits. Note: Elderella only syncs events related to your elder.

Ask Ella

Ask Ella for what you need, "When was Dad's last fall?" or "What changed this week?", and she'll pull the answer from the care story in seconds.

Get care team updates

Get a simple, scrollable digest of everything your care team has done: notes, tasks, visits, and changes. Catch up in seconds instead of piecing it together.

Medication information

Open the meds tab to see a clean, current list of medications, doses, and schedules, so you're never scrambling to remember. Set reminders so you never miss a dose.
