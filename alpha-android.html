Installing Elderella on Android

Welcome! And thank you again for being part of our Elderella Alpha group.

Android users install Elderella through Google Play, just like any other app. This being said, you are part of an internal test program and you will receive updates to internal test versions as they become available.

Please follow the steps below.

1

Download Elderella on Google Play

Click on the download link in the "Welcome to Elderella Alpha – let's get started" email you received from Elderella.

Note: As an internal tester, you will see our temporary app name when downloading Elderella.
2

Install Elderella

Once you've downloaded Elderella: Tap Install.

Elderella will be added to your phone.

Once installed, you'll see the Elderella icon on your phone screen.

Tip: Move the Elderella icon to the main screen of your phone for easy access!
3

Open Elderella and sign in

Open Elderella like any other app and sign in using your mobile phone number.
