# Request your data

> Request a copy of your personal data from Elderella. Exercise your privacy rights under GDPR and CCPA to access the information we have stored about you.

## Your right to access

Under privacy laws like GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to request a copy of your personal data. We'll provide your information in a portable format that you can download and review.

### What's included in your data export

- Your account profile and settings
- Elder profiles you manage
- Care Team member information
- Notes and journal entries
- Medications and health records
- Tasks and calendar events
- Uploaded documents and files

Your data request will be processed within 30 days as required by privacy regulations. We will send the export to the email address associated with your account.

## Request options

- **Complete data export**: All data associated with your account
- **Specific data only**: Specify which types of data you need

## Preferred formats

- JSON with HTML viewer (Recommended)
- JSON only
- PDF summary

## Contact

Questions about your data? Contact our privacy team at [privacy@elderella.com](mailto:privacy@elderella.com).
