Installing Elderella on iPhone

Welcome! And thank you again for being part of our Elderella Alpha group.

iPhone users install Elderella using TestFlight, Apple's official tool for safely testing early versions of apps. It's secure, free, and easy to use.

Please follow the steps below.

1

Install the TestFlight app

If you don't already have TestFlight on your iPhone

  • Open this link on your iPhone: apps.apple.com/app/testflight
  • Tap Get to download TestFlight.
  • Confirm you see the TestFlight icon on your iPhone's home screen.
2

Look for your Elderella TestFlight invitation

You'll receive an email invitation from TestFlight letting you know when Elderella is ready for you to install. There won't be a button in the email – this is expected.

3

Install Elderella

Once you've received the email:

  1. Open the TestFlight app on your iPhone.
  2. Look for Elderella in your list of available apps.
  3. Tap Install.

Elderella will be added to your iPhone.

Tip: Once installed, you'll see the Elderella icon on your iPhone screen (with a small yellow TestFlight dot beside it). Move the Elderella icon to the main screen of your iPhone for easy access!
4

Open Elderella and sign in

Open Elderella like any other app and sign in using your mobile phone number.
